FOLKLORE

Our Great Hall is blessed with Years of Literature, all the way from the founders until the current Inhabitors.
Get to read about the hierarchies at the Hall: That is the warden, the custodian ,the Governor, the GRC and the Hall Executives.
We honor our Anthem, "The Combatants" as it brings out the valour and courage as the Gallants loudly sing its rythmic paragraphs. During public appearances, we get to chant the war songs that granted various armies victories followed by the victory Rythms especially the one within which the name of the Governor of the Hall is added.
